How Do We Plan To Interact???

One key aspect of our IT Society will be to teach students about real world applications of computer science, how everyday software AND hardware is developed, tested, collaborated on and released into the spiderweb called the internet or the world.

Our society is specifically dedicated to assuring students are able to participate in the practical side of programming and computer science where there are projects to work together on and applying real computer science class knowledge.
